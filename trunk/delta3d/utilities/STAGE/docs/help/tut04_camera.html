Camera

      To navigate the editor, control of the camera is necessary. By default (without any additional plugins), the camera controls are as follows:
  • Left or Middle mouse button while dragging will translate the camera along the terrain (x/y plane).
  • Right mouse button while dragging will rotate the camera view.
  • Left and Right mouse buttons while dragging will translate the camera perpendicular to the current view direction.
      As an alternative to using the default camera, you can also enable additional STAGE plugins (as introduced in the previous chapter Plugins ) that will modify the control style and motion of the camera (for example, the RTS Camera plugin which will be discussed later).
